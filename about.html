My Process
  • 1. Identify Problem Space

    • Focusing on a specific Problem Space at a time, rather than multiple spaces, produces a clear solution that will guarantee success for a percentage of the target audience.

  • 2. Validate Assumptions

    • All Ideas get diluted by assumptions the longer the process goes. Having a fixed schedule of when to get feedback prevents Assumption Backlogs.

  • 3. Investigate Variables

    • Acknowledging differences is just part of the iteration process.
